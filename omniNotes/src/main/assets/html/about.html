Give a look at Google+ Comunity for Beta releases and at developer's personal mini-site for other softwares, libraries and trashwares!

Translations

ON is currently translated in:

Arabic, Chinese (China and Taiwan), Dutch, French, Galician, German, Hebrew, Italian, Lao, Portugese (Brazil and Portugal), Turkish, Russian, Slovak, Spanish, Swedish, Ukrainian

The translation project itself is opened to everyone wants to help, join the community of (great) translators on Crowdin!
